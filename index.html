The new way of Voting

This iniatiative is to enable people to vote without the hassle of visiting the polling booth. T&C apply. The tutorial is given below, follow the steps and get started with online voting.

STEP 1:

Visit your nearest ECI office and get yourself registered.

STEP 2:

Login using your Voter ID and password which has been provided to you at the time of registration.

STEP 3:

After login, proceed for location and age verification.

STEP 4:

Proceed for facial verification and wait until verified.

STEP 5:

Finally, cast your vote.
